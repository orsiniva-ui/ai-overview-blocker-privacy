AI Overview Blocker Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

AI Overview Blocker removes Google's AI Overview from Google Search results.

Information Collection

AI Overview Blocker does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Data Storage

The extension stores only one setting using Chrome Storage: whether the extension is enabled or disabled. This information stays within the user's browser or Chrome account and is never transmitted to the developer.

Third Parties

No user data is shared with third parties.
